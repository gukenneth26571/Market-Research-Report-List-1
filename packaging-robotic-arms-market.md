Packing robotic arms specialize in assembling products into cartons or boxes, ensuring they are securely packaged. Palletizing robotic arms automate the stacking of products onto pallets for storage or shipment, optimizing the organization and transportation of goods while enhancing operational efficiency across industries.</p></p>
