<p><p>The Two-Wheeler Position Sensor market includes various types such as Capacitive, Magnetic, Infrared (IR), Force Sensor, and others. Capacitive sensors detect changes in capacitance to determine position, offering high precision. Magnetic sensors use magnetic fields for contactless position detection, ensuring durability. Infrared (IR) sensors rely on light beams to measure position changes, suitable for compact designs. Force sensors detect mechanical pressure or load, ideal for applications requiring force feedback. Other sensor types cater to niche requirements, enhancing versatility in two-wheeler systems.</p></p>
