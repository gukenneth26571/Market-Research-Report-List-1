<p><p>The dural sealants market is primarily divided into two key types: synthetic polymer-based sealants and natural polymer-based sealants. Synthetic polymer-based sealants, such as those made from silicone, polyurethane, or acrylics, offer strong adhesion, flexibility, and durability, making them suitable for various applications in construction and healthcare. In contrast, natural polymer-based sealants, derived from plant or animal sources, provide biocompatibility and are increasingly favored in medical applications for their non-toxic properties. Each type serves distinct needs in industries like construction and healthcare.</p></p>
