The Multi-effect Distillation (MED) process uses multiple stages of evaporation to produce freshwater efficiently, while Multi-stage Flash (MSF) desalination involves rapid flashing of seawater at various pressures for steam generation. Both methods are critical in addressing water scarcity in arid regions, contributing to the IWPP market by providing sustainable solutions for energy and potable water production.</p></p>
