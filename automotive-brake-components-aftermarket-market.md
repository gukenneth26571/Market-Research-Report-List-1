<p><p>The automotive brake components aftermarket includes various parts essential for vehicle safety and performance. Brake pads provide friction to stop the vehicle, while brake rotors (discs) work with pads to dissipate heat. Brake shoes are used in drum brakes, pressing against the drum to slow the vehicle. Brake drums house the shoes and rotate with the wheels. Brake calipers clamp the pads onto the rotors. Brake hoses transport hydraulic fluid, enabling brake activation. These components are critical for maintaining braking efficiency and safety.</p></p>
