<p><p>The liquid flow screed market comprises two main types: pumpable calcium sulfate screed and pumpable cement-based screed. Pumpable calcium sulfate screed offers rapid installation and smooth finishes, making it ideal for underfloor heating systems. Its excellent thermal conductivity enhances energy efficiency. Conversely, pumpable cement-based screed is known for its strength and durability, suitable for high-traffic areas. Both types provide benefits such as reduced drying times and minimal installation effort, catering to diverse construction and flooring needs.</p></p>
