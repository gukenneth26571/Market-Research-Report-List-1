<p><p>The heat-sensitive adhesive label market is categorized into two main types: non-reversible and reversible. Non-reversible labels permanently bond when heat is applied, making them ideal for applications requiring strong adherence, such as product packaging and security seals. In contrast, reversible labels allow for removal and repositioning without damage, making them suitable for applications like temporary labeling or promotions. Both types cater to diverse industries, offering flexibility in labeling solutions based on durability and reusability needs.</p></p>
