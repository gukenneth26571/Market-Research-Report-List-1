<p><p>The industrial nitrogen market encompasses two primary types: compressed gas and liquid nitrogen. Compressed gas is typically stored in high-pressure cylinders or tanks and used in various applications, including metal fabrication and food preservation. Liquid nitrogen, on the other hand, is nitrogen in a cryogenic state, utilized for processes like cryopreservation, cooling, and inerting. Both forms play crucial roles in industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, and food processing, catering to diverse needs and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
