<p><strong>The Automotive Tensioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automotive Belt Tensioner</li><li>Automotive Chain Tensioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive tensioner market is primarily divided into two types: automotive belt tensioners and automotive chain tensioners. Automotive belt tensioners are used in serpentine and timing belt systems to maintain proper tension, ensuring optimal engine performance and efficiency. In contrast, automotive chain tensioners are designed for timing chains, helping to regulate chain tension and reduce wear over time. Both types are critical for maintaining engine timing, reducing noise, and enhancing the overall reliability of the vehicle's powertrain.</p></p>
