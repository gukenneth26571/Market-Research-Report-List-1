<p><p>The high-temperature mechanical seal market is categorized into three main types: single end, double-sided, and multi-faceted seals. Single end seals are designed for applications where the medium operates on one side, providing efficient sealing. Double-sided seals offer enhanced reliability by sealing both sides, suitable for applications with higher pressures or temperatures. Multi-faceted seals combine various sealing technologies to ensure optimal performance in extreme conditions, making them ideal for complex industrial applications requiring robust and versatile sealing solutions.</p></p>
