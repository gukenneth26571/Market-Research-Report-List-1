The vaporized type utilizes heated hydrogen peroxide to produce a gas that effectively penetrates surfaces and eliminates pathogens, making it ideal for enclosed spaces. In contrast, the fogging type disperses a fine mist of hydrogen peroxide solution into the air, targeting larger areas and providing thorough decontamination. Both methods offer efficient solutions for infection control in healthcare and industrial settings.</p></p>
