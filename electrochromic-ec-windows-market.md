<p><strong>The Electrochromic (EC) Windows Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer Laminate EC</li><li>All-solid-state EC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrochromic (EC) windows market features two main types: polymer laminate EC and all-solid-state EC. Polymer laminate EC utilizes a flexible layer of polymers that changes color with voltage application, allowing for dynamic light control and energy efficiency. In contrast, all-solid-state EC employs rigid materials that offer enhanced durability and faster switching times, providing effective solar control. Both types contribute to energy savings and improved indoor comfort while advancing sustainable building technologies.</p></p>
