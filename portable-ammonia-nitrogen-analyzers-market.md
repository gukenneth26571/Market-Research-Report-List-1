<p><ul><li>Colorimetric Method</li><li>Electrode Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Ammonia Nitrogen Analyzers market includes two primary types: the colorimetric method and the electrode method. The colorimetric method involves chemical reactions that produce a color change proportional to ammonia concentration, allowing for direct visual or instrumental measurement. In contrast, the electrode method utilizes ion-selective electrodes to measure the electrical potential related to ammonia ions, providing quick and accurate readings. Both methods cater to various applications in environmental monitoring, agriculture, and water quality testing.</p></p>
