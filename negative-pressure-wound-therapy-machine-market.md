<p><strong>The Negative Pressure Wound Therapy Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Negative Pressure</li><li>Discontinuous Negative Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Negative Pressure Wound Therapy (NPWT) market is segmented into Continuous Negative Pressure and Discontinuous Negative Pressure systems. Continuous Negative Pressure delivers a constant vacuum to facilitate wound healing by promoting granulation tissue formation and reducing edema. In contrast, Discontinuous Negative Pressure alternates between applying and releasing negative pressure, enhancing blood flow and oxygenation during the off-phase. Both types aim to accelerate healing, reduce infection risk, and improve patient outcomes, catering to different clinical needs and patient preferences.</p></p>
