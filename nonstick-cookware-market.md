<p><strong>The Nonstick Cookware Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PTFE-based Nonstick Cookware</li><li>Ceramic-based Nonstick Cookware</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nonstick cookware market includes two primary types: PTFE-based and ceramic-based nonstick cookware. PTFE-based cookware utilizes polytetrafluoroethylene, known for its excellent nonstick properties and durability, making it a popular choice among consumers. However, concerns about its safety at high temperatures have emerged. Ceramic-based nonstick cookware, on the other hand, is made from inorganic materials, offering a more eco-friendly alternative. It provides good nonstick performance without the potential risks associated with PTFE, appealing to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
