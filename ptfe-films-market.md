<p><p>The PTFE films market is categorized into three main types: hydrophobic PTFE films, hydrophilic PTFE films, and others. Hydrophobic PTFE films repel water, making them suitable for applications requiring moisture resistance, such as in waterproof membranes and protective coatings. Hydrophilic PTFE films, on the other hand, attract water, enhancing their surface wettability, which is beneficial for filtration and medical applications. The "others" category includes specialty films that may combine properties or cater to niche applications in various industries.</p></p>
