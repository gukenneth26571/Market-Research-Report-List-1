<p><ul><li>Single Warehouse One-Way Power Station</li><li>Single Warehouse Two-Way Power Station</li><li>Double Warehouse Two-Way Power Station</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tidal power generation technology encompasses several market types based on the design and operational capabilities of power stations. The Single Warehouse One-Way Power Station harnesses tidal energy by allowing water to flow in a single direction, generating power on the outgoing tide. In contrast, the Single Warehouse Two-Way Power Station utilizes both incoming and outgoing tides for energy generation, enhancing efficiency. The Double Warehouse Two-Way Power Station employs two separate chambers to optimize power generation during both tidal phases, maximizing energy output.</p></p>
