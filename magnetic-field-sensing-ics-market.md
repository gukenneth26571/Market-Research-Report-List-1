<p><strong>The Magnetic-field Sensing ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Effect Sensing ICs</li><li>TMR Sensing ICs</li><li>AMR Sensing ICs</li><li>GMR Sensing ICs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic-field sensing IC market encompasses several types, including Hall Effect, TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance), AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance), and GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance) sensing ICs. Hall Effect ICs detect magnetic fields using the Hall effect principle, while TMR ICs leverage spin-polarized tunneling for enhanced sensitivity. AMR ICs depend on changes in electrical resistance due to magnetic field orientation, and GMR ICs exploit quantum mechanical effects to achieve higher sensitivity and efficiency. Together, these technologies cater to diverse applications, including automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
