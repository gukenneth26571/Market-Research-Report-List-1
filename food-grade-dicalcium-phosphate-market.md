<p><p>Food Grade Dicalcium Phosphate (DCP) is a calcium supplement used in food, beverages, and animal feed. It comes in two primary market types: Dibasic Anhydrous (DCPA) and Dicalcium Phosphate Dihydrate (DCPD). DCPA is a dry, free-flowing powder with high calcium content, ideal for dry mixes and fortified foods. DCPD contains water molecules, making it suitable for wet applications like beverages and dairy products. Both types enhance calcium and phosphorus levels, ensuring nutritional benefits while meeting food safety standards. The choice depends on application needs and processing conditions.</p></p>
