<p><p>The rotary drum filter market comprises two primary types: vacuum rotary drum filters and non-vacuum rotary drum filters. Vacuum rotary drum filters utilize suction to enhance the filtration process, effectively separating solids from liquids in various industries, including wastewater treatment and food processing. In contrast, non-vacuum rotary drum filters rely on gravity and natural drainage to perform filtration without additional pressure. Both types serve essential roles in improving efficiency and productivity within filtration applications across diverse sectors.</p></p>
