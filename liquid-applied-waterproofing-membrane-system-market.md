<p><p>The Liquid Applied Waterproofing Membrane System market is characterized by two primary types: NCM (Non-Modified) and NCA (Non-Cementitious Adhesive). NCM types are polymer-based, offering flexible, seamless waterproofing solutions suitable for various substrates and applications. In contrast, NCA types utilize adhesives for bonding, providing enhanced durability and adhesion properties, ideal for challenging environments. Both systems cater to diverse industry needs, emphasizing their effectiveness in preventing water ingress and prolonging the lifespan of structures.</p></p>
