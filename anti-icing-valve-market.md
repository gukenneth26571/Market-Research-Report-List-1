<p><p>The anti-icing valve market includes two primary types: electric and pneumatic. Electric anti-icing valves utilize electrical signals for precise control, allowing for quick response times and integration with automated systems. They are often favored for their reliability and low maintenance requirements. Pneumatic anti-icing valves, on the other hand, operate using compressed air, providing powerful actuation suitable for high-pressure environments.
