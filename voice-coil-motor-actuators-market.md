<p><p>Voice Coil Motor (VCM) actuators are specialized devices used for precise motion control. The Moving Coil Type VCMs consist of a coil that moves within a magnetic field, providing direct linear motion. These are typically compact and efficient, ideal for applications like camera autofocus and hard drives. In contrast, Moving Magnet Type VCMs feature a fixed coil with a moving magnet, offering high force and reliability. This type is commonly used in industries requiring robust performance, such as robotics and medical equipment.</p></p>
