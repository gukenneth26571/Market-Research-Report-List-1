<p><strong>The Rotary Incinerators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Counter Current Rotary Incinerators</li><li>Co-Current Rotary Incinerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Incinerators market is divided into two main types: Counter Current Rotary Incinerators and Co-Current Rotary Incinerators. Counter Current Rotary Incinerators operate with the combustion gases moving in the opposite direction to the feed material, promoting efficient heat exchange and complete combustion. In contrast, Co-Current Rotary Incinerators have the combustion gases flowing in the same direction as the feed, which can enhance initial heating but may result in less efficient overall combustion. Each type serves distinct waste management needs based on operational efficiency and waste characteristics.</p></p>
