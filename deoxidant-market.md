Metallic deoxidizers, such as aluminum and magnesium, effectively remove oxygen from molten metal, improving the quality and durability of products like steel and aluminum. Non-metallic deoxidizers, including silicon and carbon compounds, provide alternative methods for oxygen removal, often used in specific applications to enhance material properties. Both types play crucial roles in metallurgical processes, contributing to efficiency and product integrity in various industries.</p></p>
