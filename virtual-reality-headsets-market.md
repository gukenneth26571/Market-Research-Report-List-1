<p><strong>The Virtual Reality Headsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circumscribed Type</li><li>Integrated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Virtual Reality (VR) headset market is primarily divided into two types: Circumscribed Type and Integrated Type. The Circumscribed Type refers to headsets that require external devices or sensors for tracking and interaction, offering enhanced accuracy but necessitating elaborate setups. In contrast, the Integrated Type features all necessary components, including sensors and displays, built directly into the headset, allowing for a more portable and user-friendly experience. Both types cater to different user preferences and application needs within the VR landscape.</p></p>
