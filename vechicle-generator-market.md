<p><ul><li>Direct Current Generator</li><li>Alternator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle generator market comprises two main types: Direct Current (DC) generators and alternators. DC generators, typically used in older vehicles, produce a steady current, making them simpler but less efficient for modern power needs. Alternators, more common today, generate alternating current, which is then converted to DC, supplying power to the vehicle’s electrical systems and recharging the battery. The shift towards alternators reflects the growing demand for reliable and efficient power generation in contemporary automotive designs.</p></p>
