<p><ul><li>Drill Pipe</li><li>Heavy Drill Pipe</li><li>Drill Collar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The downhole drilling tubular market includes three primary types: drill pipe, heavy drill pipe, and drill collars. Drill pipe is the standard tubular used for transmitting rotational force and fluid to the drill bit. Heavy drill pipe, designed for deeper wells, offers enhanced strength and weight for more challenging drilling conditions. Drill collars, thicker and heavier than drill pipe, provide the necessary weight on the bit to penetrate hard formations. Together, these components optimize drilling efficiency and performance in various oil and gas operations.</p></p>
