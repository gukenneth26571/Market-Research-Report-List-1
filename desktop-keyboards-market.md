<p><p>The desktop keyboard market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical keyboards and ordinary keyboards. In contrast, ordinary keyboards, typically membrane or chiclet styles, are more common for everyday use due to their lower cost, quieter operation, and streamlined design. Both markets cater to different user preferences, influencing purchasing decisions based on performance, comfort, and budget.</p></p>
