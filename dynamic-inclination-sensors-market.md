<p><p>Dynamic inclination sensors measure the angle of tilt or inclination in various applications, such as construction, automotive, and aerospace. The market is segmented into two main types: analogue and digital sensors. Analogue type sensors provide continuous voltage or current output proportional to the tilt angle, making them suitable for real-time monitoring. In contrast, digital type sensors deliver discrete output signals, often integrating advanced features like data logging and connectivity, offering enhanced precision and ease of integration with modern digital systems.</p></p>
