<p><p>The Automotive Window Display System market includes Windshield and Combiner types. Windshield displays project information directly onto the vehicle's windshield, offering a wide field of view and seamless integration. Combiner displays use a separate transparent screen to reflect information, often found in head-up displays (HUDs), providing a compact and cost-effective solution. Both systems enhance driver safety by minimizing distractions, offering real-time navigation, speed, and alerts, but differ in design complexity, cost, and integration methods within vehicles.</p></p>
