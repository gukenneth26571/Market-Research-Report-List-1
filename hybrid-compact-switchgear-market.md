<p><p>Hybrid compact switchgear encompasses various types based on voltage levels, each serving distinct applications. High voltage switchgear operates at voltages above 36 kV, facilitating the transmission and distribution of electricity in utility and industrial settings. Medium voltage switchgear functions between 1 kV and 36 kV, commonly used in commercial and industrial operations for power distribution. Low voltage switchgear, operating below 1 kV, is essential for residential and light industrial applications, managing electrical circuits and protecting equipment from overloads and faults.</p></p>
