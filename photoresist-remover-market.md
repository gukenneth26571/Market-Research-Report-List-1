<p><strong>The Photoresist Remover Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Type</li><li>Negative Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist remover market is divided into two main types: positive and negative photoresist removers. Positive type removers work by selectively removing areas of photoresist that have been exposed to light, allowing for more intricate designs and higher resolution patterns. In contrast, negative type removers target areas that have not been exposed, preserving the exposed sections. Both types play crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing and microfabrication processes, helping to achieve precise and desired structures on substrates.</p></p>
