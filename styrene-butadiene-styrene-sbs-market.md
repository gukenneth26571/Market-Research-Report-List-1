<p><p>Styrene Butadiene Styrene (SBS) is a thermoplastic elastomer known for its rubber-like qualities, widely used in adhesives, coatings, and rubber products. The market can be categorized into oil-extended SBS and non-oil-extended SBS. Oil-extended SBS incorporates oils to enhance flexibility and processability, making it suitable for applications requiring softening and improved flow. In contrast, non-oil-extended SBS is characterized by higher purity and resilience, making it ideal for applications demanding superior mechanical properties and thermal stability.</p></p>
