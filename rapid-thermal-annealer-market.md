<p><strong>The Rapid Thermal Annealer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lamp-based</li><li>Laser-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rapid thermal annealer market is divided into lamp-based and laser-based segments. Lamp-based systems utilize high-intensity infrared lamps to quickly heat semiconductor wafers, ensuring uniform temperature distribution and efficiency in thermal processing. In contrast, laser-based annealers employ focused laser beams for precise heating, allowing for selective annealing of specific areas on a wafer. Both technologies enhance material properties, improve electrical performance, and are crucial for advanced semiconductor manufacturing processes, each catering to different operational needs and applications.</p></p>
