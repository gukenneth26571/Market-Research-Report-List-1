<p><p>Wearable computing glasses are classified into two main market types based on sensor integration: Built-In Sensor and External Sensor. Built-In Sensor glasses incorporate integrated technology that allows them to monitor metrics such as heart rate, movement, and environmental conditions directly within the eyewear. In contrast, External Sensor glasses rely on peripheral devices or sensors that connect wirelessly, enabling a versatile data collection approach. This distinction influences user experience, functionality, and potential applications in areas like fitness, healthcare, and augmented reality.</p></p>
