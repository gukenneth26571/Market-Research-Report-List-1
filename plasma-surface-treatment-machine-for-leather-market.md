<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Plasma</li><li>Vacuum Plasma</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plasma surface treatment machine for leather market includes two main types: atmospheric plasma and vacuum plasma. Atmospheric plasma operates at ambient pressure, using ionized gas to enhance surface characteristics without the need for a vacuum chamber, making it suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, vacuum plasma treatment occurs in a controlled low-pressure environment, allowing for more precise modifications at the molecular level. Both methods improve leather properties like adhesion, dye uptake, and durability, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
