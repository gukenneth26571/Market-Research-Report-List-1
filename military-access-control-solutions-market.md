<p><ul><li>Discretionary Access Control</li><li>Mandatory Access Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Military Access Control Solutions market is primarily categorized into two types: Discretionary Access Control (DAC) and Mandatory Access Control (MAC). DAC allows users to control access to their resources, providing flexibility in managing permissions. In contrast, MAC enforces strict policies determined by centralized authority, limiting user ability to alter access rights. Both systems aim to enhance security in military environments, ensuring that sensitive information and assets are protected against unauthorized access while maintaining operational efficiency.</p></p>
