<p><p>The automotive fog lights market is primarily segmented into two types: halogen lamps and LED lamps. Halogen lamps, characterized by their affordability and warm light output, provide decent visibility in foggy conditions but consume more energy and have a shorter lifespan. In contrast, LED lamps offer superior brightness, energy efficiency, and longevity, making them a preferred choice among modern vehicles. The demand for LED fog lights is increasing due to their enhanced visibility and lower environmental impact compared to traditional halogen options.</p></p>
