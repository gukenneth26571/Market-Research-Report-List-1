<p><p>The COVID-19 in-vitro diagnostics market encompasses various testing methods. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs) provide quick results, typically using lateral flow technology for antibody detection. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) is a more detailed and quantitative method that detects antibodies or antigens, suitable for research and epidemiological studies. Neutralization Assays assess the ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus, offering insights into immune response. Together, these testing methods play critical roles in disease management and public health strategies.</p></p>
