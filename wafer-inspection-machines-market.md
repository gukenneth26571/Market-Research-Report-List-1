<p><p>Wafer inspection machines are critical in semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring the quality and integrity of wafers. The optical wafer inspection market utilizes light to detect surface defects and irregularities, providing high-resolution images for analysis. In contrast, the E-beam wafer inspection market employs electron beams to achieve greater resolution and depth of analysis, targeting nanometer-scale defects. Both types are essential for maintaining high standards in wafer production, enabling manufacturers to identify potential issues early in the fabrication process.</p></p>
