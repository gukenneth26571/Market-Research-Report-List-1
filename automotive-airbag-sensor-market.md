<p><p>The Automotive Airbag Sensor Market includes **Active Sensors** and **Passive Sensors**. Active sensors continuously monitor and detect changes in vehicle dynamics, such as acceleration or deceleration, to trigger airbag deployment. They require external power and are highly responsive. Passive sensors, on the other hand, operate without external power and rely on mechanical or chemical mechanisms to detect impacts. Both types play critical roles in ensuring timely airbag activation, enhancing vehicle safety by minimizing injury risks during collisions.</p></p>
