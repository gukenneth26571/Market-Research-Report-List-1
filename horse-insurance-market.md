<p><ul><li>Lifetime Cover</li><li>Non-lifetime Cover</li><li>Accident-only</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horse insurance market types vary primarily based on coverage duration and scope. Lifetime cover provides ongoing protection for specified conditions throughout the horse's life, ensuring comprehensive care. Non-lifetime cover offers protection only during a policy year, expiring for pre-existing conditions once the policy ends. Accident-only insurance covers injuries from accidents but excludes illnesses, offering a budget-friendly option. Other market types may include specific event coverage or mortality insurance, tailored to various needs, offering flexibility for horse owners.</p></p>
