<p><p>The sweep depalletizers market is primarily divided into two types: conventional depalletizers and robotic depalletizers. Conventional depalletizers utilize mechanical systems to systematically remove layers of products from pallets, offering high speed and reliability for bulk handling. In contrast, robotic depalletizers employ robotic arms for greater flexibility and precision, allowing for handling of various product types and sizes. Both systems are essential in automating the depalletizing process, enhancing operational efficiency and reducing labor costs in manufacturing and logistics.</p></p>
