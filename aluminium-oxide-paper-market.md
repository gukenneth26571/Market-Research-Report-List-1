<p><strong>The Aluminium Oxide Paper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slurry Method</li><li>Dipping Method</li><li>Sol-gel Method</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aluminium Oxide Paper market is categorized based on manufacturing methods. The **Slurry Method** involves applying a mixture of alumina and solvents to a paper substrate, enhancing its surface properties. The **Dipping Method** entails immersing paper in a solution containing aluminium oxide, allowing for uniform coating. The **Sol-gel Method** utilizes a chemical process to produce a fine layer of alumina on paper through hydrolysis and polymerization. Lastly, **Others** encompass alternative techniques not fitting these primary categories, broadening production possibilities.</p></p>
