<p><p>The spinal retractors market includes two primary types: blade retractors and tubular retractors. Blade retractors feature flat blades that provide visibility and access to surgical sites during spinal procedures, facilitating traditional open surgeries. Tubular retractors, conversely, are designed for minimally invasive surgeries, utilizing a hollow, tube-like structure that allows surgeons to operate with smaller incisions. Both types enhance surgical precision and reduce patient recovery times, catering to the evolving needs of spinal surgery techniques.</p></p>
