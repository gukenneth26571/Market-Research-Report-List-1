<p><p>The fire axe market consists primarily of two main types: flat head fire axes and pick fire axes. Flat head fire axes feature a broad, flat blade ideal for chopping and prying, making them effective for breaking down doors and creating pathways. In contrast, pick fire axes have a pointed end, designed for piercing hard surfaces, which aids in creating ventilation holes and accessing difficult-to-reach areas. Both types are essential tools for firefighters, catering to different operational needs in emergency response situations.</p></p>
