<p><p>The Feed Microecologics market encompasses three primary types: probiotics, prebiotics, and synbiotics. Probiotics are live beneficial microorganisms that enhance gut health in animals, enhancing digestion and nutrient absorption. Prebiotics are non-digestible fibers that promote the growth of beneficial gut bacteria. Synbiotics combine both probiotics and prebiotics, synergistically supporting animal health by balancing gut microbiota. Together, these products contribute to improved feed efficiency, animal performance, and overall health, driving their demand in the livestock and aquaculture industries.</p></p>
