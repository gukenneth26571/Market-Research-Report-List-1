<p><ul><li>Compression Type</li><li>Shear Type</li><li>Compound Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vibration-absorbing adhesive market is categorized into three main types: compression type, shear type, and compound type. Compression type adhesives are designed to absorb vibrations by compressing under load, ideal for applications needing significant impact resistance. Shear type adhesives work primarily through shear forces, effectively dampening vibrations in bonded interfaces. Compound type adhesives combine both compression and shear properties, offering versatile vibration absorption in various scenarios. Each type serves distinct needs across industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
