<p><p>The tubular heaters market is divided into two main types: single-ended and double-ended. Single-ended tubular heaters feature one end sealed, making them easier to install and maintain in various applications. They are typically used in situations where space is limited. In contrast, double-ended tubular heaters have both ends open, allowing for a more uniform heat distribution and higher wattage, making them suitable for industrial applications requiring intensive heat output. Both types serve distinct needs across diverse industries.</p></p>
