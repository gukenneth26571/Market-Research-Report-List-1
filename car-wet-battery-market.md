<p><p>The car wet battery market includes two main types: serviceable and maintenance-free batteries. Serviceable batteries require periodic maintenance, such as topping up with distilled water to maintain electrolyte levels, ensuring optimal performance. They are cost-effective but demand regular attention. Maintenance-free batteries are sealed, eliminating the need for water refills or upkeep. They are more convenient, durable, and leak-proof, making them popular for modern vehicles. Both types use lead-acid technology but differ in design and user convenience, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
