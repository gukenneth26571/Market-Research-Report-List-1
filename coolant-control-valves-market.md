<p><strong>The Coolant Control Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Way</li><li>3 Way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coolant control valves market primarily features two types: 2-way and 3-way valves. 2-way valves regulate flow in a straightforward manner, either allowing or blocking coolant circulation, making them ideal for basic on/off applications. In contrast, 3-way valves manage coolant distribution more effectively by combining flow paths, enabling mixing or diverting actions. This flexibility allows for better temperature control and system efficiency, catering to diverse industrial needs in automotive and HVAC systems, thereby driving market growth.</p></p>
