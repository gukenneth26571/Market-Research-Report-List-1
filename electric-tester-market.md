<p><p>The electric tester market comprises two main types: contact-type and contactless testers. Contact-type testers require physical contact with the electrical circuit, allowing for precise measurements of voltage and current. These are commonly used in various industries for detailed assessments. In contrast, contactless testers detect electrical fields without direct contact, providing a safer option for quick checks on live wires or equipment. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring electrical safety and efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
