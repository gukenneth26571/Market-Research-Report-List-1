<p><strong>The Marine Sonar Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single beam Scanning Sonar System</li><li>Multi-beam Sonar System</li><li>Side Scan Sonar System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine sonar systems market encompasses several types designed for different applications. Single beam scanning sonar systems emit a single sound wave to measure depth and detect objects directly below the vessel. Multi-beam sonar systems use multiple beams simultaneously, providing detailed 3D images of the seafloor and underwater features. Side scan sonar systems project sonar waves away from the vessel, creating images of the seafloor and submerged objects at greater distances. Other sonar technologies include imaging sonar, synthetic aperture sonar, and forward-looking sonar, catering to specialized needs.</p></p>
