<p><strong>The Modified Bitumen (MB) Roofing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SBS Type</li><li>APP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modified Bitumen (MB) roofing consists of two primary types: SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) and APP (Atactic Polypropylene). SBS roofing offers superior flexibility and elasticity, making it ideal for environments with significant temperature fluctuations, thus enhancing durability. In contrast, APP roofing is known for its heat-resistance and UV stability, leading to long-lasting performance. Both types are applied using torch down methods or self-adhered systems, providing excellent waterproofing and protection for commercial and residential structures alike.</p></p>
