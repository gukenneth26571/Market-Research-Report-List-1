<p><p>The underground tracer wire market comprises several types that serve various applications. Copper clad steel combines the conductivity of copper with the strength of steel, providing a cost-effective solution. Stainless steel offers durability and resistance to harsh conditions, suitable for long-term installations. High Molecular Weight Polyethylene (HMWPE) is used for its insulation properties, protecting the wire from environmental factors while ensuring signal transmission integrity in underground applications.</p></p>
