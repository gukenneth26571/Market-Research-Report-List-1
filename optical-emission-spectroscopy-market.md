<p><ul><li>Arc/Spark Optical Emission Spectroscopy</li><li>Inductively Coupled Plasma Optical Emission Spectroscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Emission Spectroscopy (OES) market primarily involves two types: Arc/Spark OES and Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP) OES. Arc/Spark OES utilizes high-voltage arcs or sparks to vaporize samples, producing light emissions that reveal elemental compositions. In contrast, ICP OES employs a plasma source to excite atoms, resulting in light emission for precise elemental analysis. Both methods are vital in materials science, metallurgy, and environmental monitoring, serving various industries that require accurate chemical composition data.</p></p>
