<p><ul><li>Oil-free Screw Compressors</li><li>Oil-injected Screw Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The process gas screw compressors market is divided into two main types: oil-free screw compressors and oil-injected screw compressors. Oil-free screw compressors are designed to compress gas without lubrication oil, ensuring minimal contamination, making them ideal for applications requiring high purity. In contrast, oil-injected screw compressors utilize oil for lubrication during the compression process, offering higher efficiency and lower operational temperatures, suitable for various industrial applications where oil remains uncontaminated. Each type caters to specific operational needs and industry standards.</p></p>
