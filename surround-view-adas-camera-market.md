<p><p>The Surround View Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS) Camera market is divided into two main types: monocular and binocular cameras. Monocular cameras use a single lens to capture a wide-angle image, providing a basic 360-degree view of the vehicle's surroundings. Meanwhile, binocular cameras deploy two lenses to create depth perception, enhancing object detection and spatial awareness. Both types aim to improve safety and navigational ease, but binocular systems generally offer more advanced features, such as improved image accuracy and detail.</p></p>
