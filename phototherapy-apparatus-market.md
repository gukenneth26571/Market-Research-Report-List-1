<p><ul><li>Conventional</li><li>LED</li><li>Fiberoptic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phototherapy apparatus market encompasses three main types: conventional, LED, and fiberoptic systems. Conventional phototherapy uses fluorescent lamps to deliver broad-spectrum light for treating conditions like jaundice in neonates. LED phototherapy systems utilize light-emitting diodes, offering greater energy efficiency and targeted wavelengths, enhancing treatment outcomes. Fiberoptic phototherapy employs flexible fibers to deliver light directly to the skin, providing localized treatment with minimal heat generation and increased comfort for patients, making it suitable for sensitive cases.</p></p>
