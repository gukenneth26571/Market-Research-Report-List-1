<p><p>The automotive cooling system gasket market consists of several key types, each serving specific functions. Exhaust flange gaskets seal the exhaust system connections, preventing leaks and ensuring efficient exhaust flow. Radiator gaskets maintain proper sealing between the radiator and engine components, crucial for optimal cooling. Exhaust gaskets create tight seals at manifold connections, reducing emissions and noise. Water pump gaskets facilitate the sealing of the water pump to the engine, ensuring coolant flow remains uninterrupted, thus maintaining engine temperature stability.</p></p>
