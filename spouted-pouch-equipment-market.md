<p><p>The spouted pouch equipment market is characterized by two primary control types: pneumatic motor control and servo motor control. Pneumatic motor control utilizes compressed air to drive machinery, offering robust performance and simplicity for high-speed operations. In contrast, servo motor control employs electric motors for precise movement and flexibility, enhancing accuracy and reducing energy consumption. Both types cater to different production needs, with pneumatic systems favoring speed and durability, while servo systems prioritize precision and efficiency in pouch filling and sealing processes.</p></p>
