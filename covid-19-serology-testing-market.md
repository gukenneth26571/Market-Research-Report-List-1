<p><strong>The Covid-19 Serology Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Covid-19 serology testing market comprises three primary types: Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT), Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and Neutralization Assays. RDTs provide quick results using small blood samples, making them ideal for point-of-care settings. ELISA is a more laboratory-based approach, offering high sensitivity and specificity for detecting antibodies. Neutralization Assays assess the ability of antibodies to inhibit viral infection, providing critical insights for vaccine efficacy and immune response. Each method serves unique needs in the fight against Covid-19.</p></p>
