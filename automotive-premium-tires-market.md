<p><strong>The Automotive Premium Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive premium tires market is divided into two main types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature multiple layers of nylon or fabric placed at diagonal angles, providing a sturdy structure but less flexibility, making them more suitable for light-duty applications. In contrast, radial tires have plies arranged perpendicularly to the direction of travel, enhancing handling, fuel efficiency, and longevity. Radial tires are generally preferred for high-performance vehicles and passenger cars, driving their dominance in the premium segment.</p></p>
