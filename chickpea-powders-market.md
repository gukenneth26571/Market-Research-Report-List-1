<p><strong>The Chickpea Powders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Desi</li><li>Kabuli</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chickpea powder market comprises primarily two types: Desi and Kabuli. Desi chickpeas are smaller, darker, and often used in traditional cuisines; their powder is rich in fiber and protein, appealing to health-conscious consumers. Kabuli chickpeas, larger and lighter in color, are commonly used in salads and snacks; their powder has a milder flavor. Both types cater to diverse culinary needs, with Desi varieties favored in South Asian cooking and Kabuli varieties popular in Mediterranean and Middle Eastern dishes.</p></p>
