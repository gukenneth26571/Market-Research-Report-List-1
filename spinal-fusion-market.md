<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Spinal Fusion Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transforaminal Lumbar Interbody Fusion (TLIF)</li><li>Anterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (ALIF)</li><li>Posterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (PLIF)</li><li>Posterior Lumbar Fusion (PLF)</li><li>Extreme Lateral Interbody Fusion (XLIF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal fusion market encompasses various surgical techniques aimed at stabilizing the spine. Transforaminal Lumbar Interbody Fusion (TLIF) involves accessing the spine from the side to address nerve compression. Anterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (ALIF) accesses the spine from the front, ideal for significant disc degeneration. Posterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (PLIF) is performed from the back, allowing for direct disc removal. Posterior Lumbar Fusion (PLF) fuses vertebrae without interbody implants, while Extreme Lateral Interbody Fusion (XLIF) accesses the spine through the side, minimizing muscle damage. Each method addresses specific spinal conditions and patient needs.</p></p>
