<p><p>The Mid-and High-Level Precision GPS Receiver market is segmented into Single Frequency and Dual Frequency types. Single Frequency receivers utilize a single GPS signal, typically providing basic navigation and positioning capabilities ideal for general applications. In contrast, Dual Frequency receivers use two different frequencies, enhancing accuracy and reducing errors caused by atmospheric conditions. This makes Dual Frequency receivers suitable for more demanding applications, such as surveying and precision agriculture, where higher accuracy and reliability are essential.</p></p>
