<p><p>The fix speed peristaltic pump market is segmented into peristaltic tube pumps and peristaltic hose pumps. Peristaltic tube pumps use flexible tubing to create a seal, effectively moving liquids with low shear and pulsation, ideal for gentle dosing in applications like pharmaceuticals. Conversely, peristaltic hose pumps utilize a hose to handle more viscous fluids and are suited for demanding industrial applications, offering higher flow rates and pressure capabilities. Both types ensure precise fluid handling, making them essential in various sectors.</p></p>
