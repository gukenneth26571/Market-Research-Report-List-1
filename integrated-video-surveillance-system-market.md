<p><p>The Integrated Video Surveillance System market includes two primary types: Network Video Recorder (NVR) Systems and Digital Video Recorder (DVR) Systems. NVR systems utilize IP cameras and network protocols for remote viewing and cloud storage, offering flexibility and scalability for modern surveillance needs. In contrast, DVR systems are designed for analog cameras, capturing and storing video locally on hard drives. While DVRs are often cost-effective for smaller setups, NVRs provide enhanced features and integration for larger, more dynamic environments.</p></p>
