<p><p>Superalloys are high-performance materials essential for aerospace applications, categorized into three primary types: iron-based, nickel-based, and cobalt-based superalloys. Iron-based superalloys are suitable for lower temperature applications and offer good strength and oxidation resistance. Nickel-based superalloys excel in high-temperature environments, providing exceptional creep resistance and thermal stability, making them ideal for turbine engines. Cobalt-based superalloys, known for their excellent wear resistance and ability to withstand extreme environments, are often used in critical aerospace components where durability and strength are paramount.</p></p>
