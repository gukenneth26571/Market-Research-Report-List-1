<p><strong>The Full-lamination Optical Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OCA</li><li>OCR</li><li>SCA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Full-Lamination Optical Film market includes three primary types: OCA (Optically Clear Adhesive), OCR (Optically Clear Resin), and SCA (Self-adhesive Clear Adhesive). OCA is used for bonding displays, providing clarity and durability. OCR offers enhanced optical properties, often suitable for high-performance applications. SCA, on the other hand, features a self-adhesive backing, simplifying installation. These films are crucial for improving display quality in devices like smartphones, tablets, and LCD screens, enhancing visual performance and device durability.</p></p>
