<p><p>The solar panel bonding adhesives market primarily features two types: epoxy and polyurethane. Epoxy adhesives are known for their high strength, durability, and resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for long-lasting solar panel installations. On the other hand, polyurethane adhesives offer flexibility and excellent adhesion to various substrates, ensuring effective bonding even under thermal expansion. Both types cater to different application needs, enhancing the performance and longevity of solar panels in diverse environmental conditions.</p></p>
