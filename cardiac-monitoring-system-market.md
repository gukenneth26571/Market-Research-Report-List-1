<p><strong>The Cardiac Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Holter Monitors</li><li>Implantable Loop Recorder</li><li>Cardiac Output Monitoring Devices</li><li>Cardiac Rhythm Management Devices(CRM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac monitoring system market comprises several key types. Holter monitors are portable devices that continuously record heart activity over 24-48 hours. Implantable loop recorders are small devices implanted under the skin for long-term heart rhythm monitoring. Cardiac output monitoring devices assess the amount of blood the heart pumps, essential for managing heart failure. Cardiac rhythm management devices (CRM), including pacemakers and defibrillators, help regulate heartbeats, ensuring proper heart function and preventing arrhythmias.</p></p>
