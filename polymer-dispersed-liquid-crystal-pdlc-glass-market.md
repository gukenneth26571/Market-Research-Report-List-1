<p><p>Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC) Glass is a smart glass technology that combines liquid crystals with a polymer matrix to create a tunable transparency feature. When an electrical current is applied, the liquid crystals align, allowing light to pass through and rendering the glass transparent. When the current is switched off, the liquid crystals scatter, turning the glass opaque. This innovative technology is widely utilized in architectural applications, automotive displays, and privacy windows due to its ability to control light transmission and enhance aesthetics.</p><p>The PDLC Glass Market is experiencing robust growth driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient building solutions and advancements in smart technologies.
